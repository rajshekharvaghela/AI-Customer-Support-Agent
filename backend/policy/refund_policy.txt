# REFUND POLICY DOCUMENT

## Overview
This document outlines the strict refund policy for all e-commerce transactions.

## Key Rules

1. REFUND WINDOW
   - Standard refunds: Within 30 days of purchase
   - Electronics: Within 14 days of purchase
   - Final sale items: NO refunds allowed under any circumstances

2. REFUND AMOUNT RULES
   - Refunds under $100: Automatic approval
   - Refunds $100-$500: Requires review; approve if policy compliant
   - Refunds over $500: MANDATORY HUMAN ESCALATION - agent must refuse

3. PRODUCT CATEGORY RESTRICTIONS
   - Clearance items: NOT eligible for refund
   - Final sale items: NOT eligible for refund (explicitly marked)
   - Perishables: NOT eligible for refund
   - Custom/personalized items: NOT eligible for refund

4. CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
   - Item must be in original, unused condition
   - Packaging must be intact
   - If item shows signs of use: DENY refund

5. ESCALATION TRIGGERS
   - Refund amount > $500
   - VIP customer status (register_date before 2022)
   - Multiple refunds from same customer (>3 in last 6 months)
   - Suspicious patterns or aggressive language

## Denial Scenarios
- Final sale items: Always deny
- Outside refund window: Always deny
- Item marked as used/opened: Always deny
- Over $500 without escalation: Always deny
- Clearance or personalized items: Always deny

## Approval Scenarios
- Within refund window AND within amount limits AND item condition acceptable: APPROVE
- Policy compliant with human escalation completed: APPROVE
